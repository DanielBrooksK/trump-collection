The Trump Collection

A Legacy Built in Steel, Gold, and Vision

› Collection

For decades, the Trump brand has defined the pinnacle of American luxury — from sky-defining towers on Manhattan's West Side to championship golf courses on the Atlantic coast of Scotland. Each property, each enterprise, bears the hallmark of uncompromising excellence and an unwavering commitment to the finest in architecture, hospitality, and experience.

The Trump Collection spans hotels, golf resorts, commercial real estate, fine wine, media, and technology. It is a legacy built not in words, but in steel, marble, glass, and turf — visible on skylines and coastlines across the world.

World-Class Hospitality

Trump Hotels

Trump Hotels redefine the standard of luxury hospitality. Forbes Five-Star rated properties feature floor-to-ceiling windows, marble bathrooms, Michelin-starred dining, and the signature Trump Attaché® personalized service. Every property is an architectural statement — bold, commanding, and impossible to ignore.

From the 92-story tower in Chicago to the 24-karat gold glass exterior in Las Vegas, from the storied grandeur of Turnberry in Scotland to the tropical elegance of Trump National Doral in Miami — each hotel raises the standard for every guest, every stay, every time.

Championship Golf

Trump Golf

Twenty-one world-class championship courses across the globe, each selected and developed for its extraordinary natural setting and potential for greatness. Trump Golf courses consistently rank among the finest in the world, demanding the best from every player while rewarding them with breathtaking landscapes.

Turnberry in Scotland has hosted four Open Championships. Doonbeg on the wild Atlantic coast of Ireland commands views that simply cannot be replicated. Bedminster in New Jersey and Doral in Florida represent the pinnacle of American tournament golf — home to LIV Golf invitational events and generations of memorable competition.

The Parent Company

Trump Organization

The Trump Organization is the parent enterprise encompassing all Trump brand ventures — from hospitality and golf to commercial real estate and beyond. Spanning decades of iconic development, the Organization has shaped skylines, transformed coastlines, and established a global standard of excellence in premium real estate and luxury experiences.

Its portfolio of commercial properties, residential developments, and landmark towers represents one of the most recognizable real estate brands on earth. The Trump Organization is synonymous with quality, ambition, and results that define entire city landscapes.

Fine Wine & Estate

Trump Winery

Nestled in Charlottesville, Virginia — among the most celebrated wine-producing regions on the East Coast — Trump Winery produces award-winning wines from historic estate grounds that have been cultivated for generations. The property combines Old World tradition with New World ambition.

From crisp Chardonnays to rich Cabernet Francs and elegant sparkling wines, each bottle reflects the terroir of the Blue Ridge foothills and the meticulous standards of winemakers committed to excellence. Trump Winery is Virginia's largest estate winery and a proud embodiment of American agricultural heritage.

Media & Technology

Trump Media & Technology Group

Trump Media & Technology Group (TMTG) represents President Trump's entry into the technology and media sector — built on the foundational principle that free speech is non-negotiable. Truth Social, TMTG's flagship social media platform, provides an uncensored, open environment where Americans can speak freely, connect authentically, and engage without ideological gatekeeping.

Truth+, the platform's streaming service, extends that commitment to video content — providing an alternative to mainstream media with bold, independent programming. TMTG reflects a broader mission: that the most powerful nation on earth should never silence the voices of its own citizens.
