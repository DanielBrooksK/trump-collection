Spring Sights

A Season of Discovery at Trump Collection Properties

Spring is the finest season for travel — and Trump Collection properties make it unforgettable. From Miami's sun-drenched fairways to the dramatic Scottish coastline in bloom, here is your definitive spring guide.

The Season's Finest

Where to Go This Spring

Whether you are seeking warmth, adventure, coastal grandeur, or city sophistication, Trump Collection properties offer the ideal setting for spring travel. Each destination has been curated for the discerning traveler who accepts nothing less than extraordinary.

Miami, Florida
Trump National Doral

Spring in Miami is perfection — warm sun, tropical breezes, and the legendary Blue Monster course at its finest. Trump National Doral comes alive in spring with championship-caliber fairways, world-class spa facilities, and the vibrant energy that only Miami can deliver.

Spring Highlight: The Blue Monster course plays its best from March through May — cooler mornings, lush conditions, ideal scoring weather.
New York City, New York
Trump International Hotel & Tower

Overlooking Central Park at Columbus Circle, Trump International Hotel & Tower offers front-row seats to one of the world's great spring spectacles. Cherry blossoms, green promenades, and the electric renewal of the city's greatest park — all from the comfort of a Forbes Five-Star suite.

Spring Highlight: Request a Central Park view suite in April for the peak cherry blossom bloom — a once-a-year sight from an incomparable vantage point.
Ayrshire, Scotland
Trump Turnberry

Turnberry in spring is among the most magnificent sights in golf. As the gorse turns golden and the Ailsa Craig rises from the Firth of Clyde, the world's most storied links course awakens to its annual prime. Four Open Championships testify to what makes this coastline peerless.

Spring Highlight: The Ailsa Course plays differently every spring morning — arrive early to experience the course in low mist before the sun burns through.
Honolulu, Hawaii
Trump International Hotel Waikiki

Spring in Waikiki means calmer seas, perfect temperatures, and the island at its most serene before summer crowds arrive. Trump International Hotel Waikiki provides the ideal base — Forbes Five-Star amenities, Pacific Ocean panoramas, and access to the finest dining and experiences the islands offer.

Spring Highlight: Spring brings ideal whale-watching conditions — humpback whales are frequently visible from higher-floor ocean-view rooms through May.
